District 6

If you aren't sure where District 6 is, you're not alone. District boundaries are highly irregular and these boundaries change as the legislative maps are updated. Although the frequent changes are happening as the courts force congressional maps to be more fair (which is a good thing!) a casualty of this process is that people have no idea who represents them and which districts they live in.

The map below shows (as of 2024) where District 6 is located.

For many folks, this is still not enough information to determine whether you live in District 6. If you go to https://findmydistrict.ohiosos.gov you can put in your address and see for sure what your Board of Education district is.
